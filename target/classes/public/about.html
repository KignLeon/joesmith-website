The "Swiss Army Knife"

Servant Leader. Educator. Father.

Joe Smith is a graduate of Hampton University with a degree in sociology, bringing a unique lens to understanding people, cultures, and the demographics that impact a community. With more than 15 years of service in K-12 education, Joe has earned a reputation as a "Swiss Army Knife"—a multi-talented public servant, educator, and advocate who strengthens every community he touches.

Guided by faith, integrity, and a commitment to excellence, Joe is a father on a mission, showing up with passion, purpose, and a "whatever it takes" mindset. Now residing in San Diego, Joe brings a statewide perspective gained from serving communities across northern, central, and southern California.

His leadership is grounded in service, empathy, and the belief that students and families deserve unwavering advocacy and access to opportunity.
